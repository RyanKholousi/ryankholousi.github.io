The Jewell

WordPress, Development

Summary

The Jewell is Omaha’s newest jazz club and restaurant. I was hired on during the final stages of construction on their website to get it wrapped up quickly before grand opening. The branding and a couple pages of their website were built out by another designer, so my freelance partner, Danny, were asked to integrate the site for taking online credit card payments, event posting capabilities, and the overall process of online ticket sales. I had never worked with credit card payments or managing events but I was up for the challenge. We had a successful on time launch with ticket sales climbing steadily ever since.
